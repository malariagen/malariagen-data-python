## 📚 **Complete Educational Suite Created**

### **📖 Tutorial 1: Getting Started with MalariaGEN**
**File**: `01_getting_started.ipynb`  
**Target Audience**: Absolute beginners  
**Content**: 500+ lines of educational content

**Learning Objectives:**
- 🌍 Understanding MalariaGEN and its global health importance
- 📊 Accessing mosquito (Ag3) and parasite (Pf7) datasets
- 🔍 Basic data exploration and visualization techniques
- 📈 Creating meaningful plots and geographic distributions
- 🎯 Real-world context for malaria research

**Unique Features:**
- Step-by-step explanations with no prior knowledge assumed
- Hands-on code with detailed comments
- Real data examples from actual MalariaGEN datasets
- Visual learning with comprehensive plots and maps

### **🧬 Tutorial 2: Basic Genetic Analysis**
**File**: `02_basic_genetic_analysis.ipynb`  
**Target Audience**: Beginners with basic genetics knowledge  
**Content**: 600+ lines of scientific content

**Learning Objectives:**
- 🧬 Understanding genetic variations (SNPs, INDELs, CNVs)
- 📊 Accessing and analyzing SNP data from mosquitoes and parasites
- 🔍 Population genetics concepts and diversity metrics
- 📈 Visualizing genetic diversity across populations
- 🎯 Applications in malaria research and control

**Unique Features:**
- Bridges basic data access to scientific analysis
- Real genetic data exploration with population genetics
- Comprehensive diversity analysis with visualizations
- Links to real-world research applications

### **💊 Tutorial 3: Drug Resistance Analysis**
**File**: `03_drug_resistance_analysis.ipynb`  
**Target Audience**: Intermediate learners  
**Content**: 700+ lines of advanced content

**Learning Objectives:**
- 💊 Understanding the global drug resistance crisis
- 🧬 Key resistance genes (kelch13, pfcrt, pfmdr1, dhps, dhfr)
- 📊 Detecting resistance markers in genomic data
- 🌍 Geographic mapping of resistance patterns
- 📈 Tracking resistance trends over time
- 🚨 Public health implications and WHO standards

**Unique Features:**
- Critical public health application with real-world impact
- WHO resistance threshold classification
- Geographic resistance mapping with visualization
- Simulated resistance analysis demonstrating real techniques
